Hidden Moth Game

A game about a moth hiding from light, after seeing its family get brutally murdered. This was a group project for the same Love2D course that Heart Attack Hustle is from, it is also a submission for 2023 Spring - EasternJam, the theme was Hidden, Hence why this moth isn't attracted to the light.
