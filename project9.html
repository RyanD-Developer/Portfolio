Village Guardian: Chronicles of the Portal

Village Guardian is a 2D pixel platformer game. It was developed with paid assets since Deven is a mostly 3D artist and Ashton and I are not at all an artist, it was made in the unity engine, for the final project in my college course. I was in charge of player movement, the bosses and enemies scripts, and some level layouts/designs. This is a big project that I loved working on, as I have always wanted to make a game like this and I enjoyed developing the game so much that I'm hoping to update more in the future with (hopefully) more than 9 levels.
