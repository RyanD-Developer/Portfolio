Heart Attack Hustle

Heart Attack Hustle is a 2D endless side-scroller. It was developed from scratch for a project in my college course, using Love2D. I was in charge of collisions, hazards, and the endless scrolling function. This was a fun game to make as the character and some of the hazards are based on the show JoJo's Bizzare Adventure, a show that my peers and I like to watch.
